ABOUT US

Welcome to our site! For over 20 years people have been telling us that the cookies made by "The Matriarch" could be sold to the masses, and we have always agreed. In April of 2014 we finally decided to begin packaging up these delicious treats and start selling them to the sweet-toothed cookie consumers. We believe that people who truly love cookies have a developed, sophisticated taste, and our cookies are more than up to the challenge! We are based in Maryland, just outside of Washington, DC, and many, many people in this area have fallen in love with our cookies, so we are excited to see what happens when we actually start selling them! Try some today and let us know what you think!
